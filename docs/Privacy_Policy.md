## Information We Collect

We only collect and store the information necessary to provide the app's core functionality. All data collection happens only through your explicit actions inside the app.

### Information You Provide Directly
- **Location Data**: GPS coordinates, addresses, and location names you save
- **Personal Notes**: Text descriptions and notes you add to saved places
- **Photos**: Images you attach to your saved locations
- **Categories**: Custom category names you create
- **Website Links**: URLs you associate with saved places

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Location**: GPS coordinates when you choose to save your current location
- **Device Information**: Device model, operating system version, and app version
- **Usage Data**: How you interact with the App (anonymized)

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- **Provide Core Functionality**: Save, organize, and display your favorite places
- **Enable Navigation**: Integrate with mapping services (Google Maps, Waze, Apple Maps)
- **Facilitate Sharing**: Allow you to share location information with others
- **Backup & Sync**: Enable data backup and restoration
- **Improve the App**: Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience

## Data Storage and Security

The app does not transmit your personal location data to our servers. All location-related data remains on your device unless you explicitly back it up or share it.

### Local Storage
- Your location data is primarily stored locally on your device
- Photos and notes are stored in the app's secure local database
- You maintain full control over your data

### Cloud Backup (Optional)
- If you choose to enable backup, encrypted data may be stored securely
- Backups are encrypted and accessible only to you
- You can disable cloud backup at any time

### Security Measures
- Data encryption both in transit and at rest
- Secure authentication protocols
- Regular security updates and monitoring

## Data Sharing

### We DO NOT Share Your Personal Data With Third Parties For:
- Advertising purposes
- Marketing campaigns
- Data selling or monetization

### We May Share Data Only When:
- **You Choose to Share**: When you actively share a location with friends
- **Legal Requirements**: If required by law or legal process

### Third-Party Services
- **Mapping Services**: Location data is shared with mapping apps (Google Maps, Waze) only when you choose to navigate
- **Import/Export**: Data sharing occurs only when you actively import from or export to other apps

## Your Rights and Choices

### You Have the Right To:
- **Access**: View all data stored in the app
- **Edit**: Modify or update your saved places and information
- **Delete**: Remove individual locations or all data
- **Export**: Download your data in a portable format
- **Control Location Access**: Enable/disable GPS access at any time

### Data Retention
- Data is retained as long as you keep it in the app
- Deleted locations are permanently removed from your device
- Backup data is deleted when you disable backup or uninstall the app

## Location Services

### GPS Usage

## Security Approach

**MySpotiFIX is designed with privacy and security as core principles:**

- **Local-First Architecture**: Your data stays on your device, reducing exposure to external threats
- **No Central Server Dependency**: We don't maintain a central database of user locations, eliminating single points of failure
- **User-Controlled Data**: You decide what to save, share, and delete - we never access your data without your explicit action
- **Minimal Attack Surface**: With no cloud storage by default, there are fewer opportunities for unauthorized access

While we implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. However, our local-first approach significantly reduces security risks compared to cloud-based alternatives. 

In the event of a security incident:
- We will investigate immediately
- Affected users will be notified promptly
- We will take steps to prevent future incidents
- We are not liable for damages resulting from unauthorized third-party access beyond our reasonable control
